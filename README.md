# Aegisub Scripts
Aegisub scripts I made for my own convenience.

## add Symbol

Adds a styled symbol at the start of all selected lines.

This is intended for karaokes involving group singing (3+ singers) so that you may have a colored symbol. The default symbols available are the following: ♦, ♥, ♠, ♣, ●, ♪, ♫, ▲, ▼, ■. You may modify the code to add more.

### Guide

> #### Step 1
> Time your karaoke and set the styles for each singer. You can use the actor field to write down when multiple people sing together.
> <img width="630" height="175" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5938e0d3-42c0-4f57-87e6-4079ddd462c6" />
> #### Step 2
> Create a style for each singer's symbol making sure the primary and secondary colors are the same. You can also change the size to make the symbol more visible.
> <img width="852" height="934" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/41fad0b0-65f8-4211-9a31-6b0f30366135" />
> #### Step 3
> Assign a symbol to each singer, you can make a guide at the start of the karaoke so you don't forget who got assigned what. This also lets you see if you need to resize symbols or not.
> <img width="1920" height="591" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ac74d57a-2cff-467b-a43f-750cb64085f2" />
> #### Step 4
> Select all the lines belonging to a specific singer where you want a symbol. You may choose to only add symbols to group lines or to all their lines, keep it consistent to not confuse people. Sorting the lines by style or actor makes the selection process faster.
> <img width="1180" height="857" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9445288e-4961-4d18-bdcc-ba7303b88e22" />
> Then repeat for all the other singers.
> <img width="1180" height="857" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0b18c3f0-778a-488f-9cd0-1c80a943c897" />
> If a line is too long, you may split it and copy-paste the beginning
> <img width="1180" height="857" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bc3db349-0d3d-4d66-b264-94873a5938e8" />
> <img width="1180" height="857" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/166117df-ffc0-4ec7-906e-07c83808ec8b" />
> #### Step 5
> Sort lines by start time if you sorted them by style/actor before, apply the karaoke template, enjoy your karaoke and dive deeper into idol hell.
> <img width="1180" height="857" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/22ac2639-b815-4a81-be91-9ceeffc58e74" />
